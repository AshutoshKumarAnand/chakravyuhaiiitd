Putnam Pens (PP) launched their new marker on Sunday. It was the most successful product launch in the history of luxury pens. However this dream launch turned into a nightmare when several users of the marker complained of headache and fever. Lab tests found that the marker contained high levels of a certain type of toxin which lead to an outbreak of brain fever. The disease started with a seizure which lead to the mutation of a certain gene. Several people went into a coma. The authorities are in a state of shock. Only way out of this for the people who are carrying this mutated gene seems to be a cure which can potentially end their lives if it goes wrong. The authorities feel that this fatal cure is an acceptable risk that may have to be taken in order to get over this strange disease.
